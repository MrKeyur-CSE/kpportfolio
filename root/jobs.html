Introduction

Many youngsters are graduating every year and for freshers like them, it is not quite easy to get a job. Therefore, we are going to provide a platform where people can apply for jobs as per their requirements from their homes only. Moreover, for companies or shops, finding an employee is tough. By using this platform, they can offer employment based on employee resumes and then they can ask for an interview.

Role in Project

This was a group project of 2 people. I am interested in UI/UX so the whole project's UI and flow were done by me. And I was mainly involved in the development part. We've made it in android using JAVA and iOS with Swift. I will be working with my teammate, I worked on back-end development and some portion of frontend. (i.e. XML, Java and Swift files). and other worked in frontend.

Our Process

We started with the project propsal to our college. Afterward we started working on our wireframes and UI/UX designs with the technical and functional specification. Now we will start working on frontend and backend.
